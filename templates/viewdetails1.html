Bangladesh National Museum

About the Museum

The Bangladesh National Museum is a must-visit for history lovers. The museum houses a rich collection of artifacts that offer insights into the country's rich past. From ancient coins and sculptures to traditional musical instruments and textiles, the museum has something for everyone. The museum is located in the heart of Dhaka, and is easily accessible by public transport. The admission fee is very reasonable, and the staff is friendly and helpful. I highly recommend a visit to the Bangladesh National Museum!
